Jungle Chess

A two-player strategy game where animals fight by rank and try to reach the opponent's den.

How to Play

  • Players take turns moving one animal horizontally or vertically.
  • Animals capture enemy animals of equal or lower rank.
  • The Rat can capture the Elephant, but the Elephant cannot capture the Rat.
  • Only Rats may enter rivers. Lions and Tigers can jump across clear rivers.
  • Animals standing in enemy traps can be captured by any opposing animal.
  • Move any animal into the opponent's den to win.
